Rigging and Lifting Safety

Considerable safety concerns must be taken into account for both the users of the various "lifting" equipment and the nearby workers. Before each shift, rigging equipment for material handling must be examined to guarantee safety. An expert person chosen by the employer must check the sling, all fastenings and all attachments each day before use for damage or flaws. A company can complete its commitments to adhere to regulatory standards and the operational control requirements of ISO 45001:2018 by using rigging and lifting safety.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND?

* lifting and rigging Operators / maintenance personnel

* Contractors

* independent contractors

* Supervisors and Managers

* Management experts and consultants

* Anyone with an interest in electrical safety
